Kitview Search v1.0

Complete user guide for the multilingual search application

📋 1. Overview

Kitview Search is a multilingual web search application for querying an orthodontic patient database. The interface adapts to all screens (PC, tablet, mobile) and offers two usage levels:

  • Simple mode: clean interface for immediate use
  • Advanced mode: full access to configuration and analysis tools

🔍 3. Basic Usage

3.1 Performing a Search

  1. Click in the search box in the center of the screen
  2. Type your query in natural language
  3. Launch the search by clicking ⬆️ or pressing Enter
💡 Query Examples patients with open bite • class II division 1 • adults with crowding • children under 12 years old

3.2 Voice Search 🎤

The microphone button offers two modes:

Action Mode Description
Single click Normal Single listen, searches after 5 sec of silence
Long press Hands-free Extended listening, say "Kitview" + your search
Hands-free mode Say "Kitview patients with bruxism under 30 years old" to automatically launch the search.

3.3 Side Panel

The left panel offers:

  • ✨ New search: Resets the interface
  • Recent conversations: Search history (click to copy, ▶ to rerun)
  • Examples: Predefined search suggestions
💡 Tip The right border of the panel can be resized by drag and drop.

3.4 Displaying Results

Results are displayed as patient cards containing:

  • Last name and first name
  • Age and gender
  • Date of birth
  • Pathologies (colored tags)

Click on a card to see the complete patient details.

📊 6. Analytics Page

Access: click 📊 in the toolbar or from settings

6.1 Statistics Cards

At the top of the page, cards display key indicators: total searches, average response time, success rate, distribution by mode.

6.2 Filters

Filter Description
PeriodStart and end date
Modequick, ai, compare, union
EngineFilter by AI engine
SearchText filtering

6.3 Available Views

"AI Summary" View

Global synthesis with general statistics, optimization recommendations and top searched terms.

"Cards" View

Grid display of performed searches. Each card shows the question, number of results, response time, and a ▶ button to rerun.

"Stats" View

Detailed charts:

  • Searches per day (bars)
  • Results with/without patients (donut)
  • Distribution by mode (horizontal bars)
  • Average response time per day (curve)
  • Top 10 searched terms (bars)
  • Distribution by AI engine (donut)

⌨️ 7. Shortcuts and Tips

7.1 Keyboard Shortcuts

7.2 Tips

  • Autocomplete: The search field offers suggestions based on history
  • Resizable panel: Drag the right border of the side panel
  • Patient cards: Click to expand, click again to collapse
  • Automatic theme: "Auto" mode follows system preferences
