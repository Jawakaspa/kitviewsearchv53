🔍 Searching the Manual

The search bar at the top of the screen allows you to instantly find any information in the manual.

How to search?

  1. Click in the search bar or press Ctrl + F
  2. Type your search (e.g., "import photos")
  3. Results appear instantly, highlighted in yellow
  4. Use the ◀ Prev and Next ▶ buttons to navigate between results
💡 Tip: Use the section filter to limit the search to a specific chapter of the manual.

🤖 The AI Assistant

The AI assistant is your personal guide to navigate the manual. Ask it your questions in natural language!

🤖

Kitview Assistant

Hello! I'm here to help you use Kitview. Ask me your questions, for example:

How do I import photos? Create a new patient Export to PDF

How to use the assistant?

  1. Click the 💬 button at the bottom right of the screen
  2. Type your question in the text area
  3. The assistant analyzes the manual and responds with links to relevant sections
  4. Click the links to go directly to the documentation
💡 Tip The assistant responds in the manual's language. If you're viewing the English version, ask your questions in English for better results.

Example questions

  • "How do I create a new patient in Kitview?"
  • "What's the difference between the viewers?"
  • "How do I export photos to my computer?"
  • "How do I customize the interface colors?"

📚 Manual Versions

The manual exists in 4 versions to adapt to your level of expertise. Use the colored chips at the top of the screen to switch between versions.

📘

Standard

Complete version with all information

🟢

Beginner

Detailed explanations, simple analogies, step by step

🟡

Intermediate

For users who have the basics

🔴

Expert

Essentials only, for advanced users

💡 Tip: Start with the Standard version. If you find the explanations too technical, switch to the Beginner version. If you already master Kitview, the Expert version will save you time.
